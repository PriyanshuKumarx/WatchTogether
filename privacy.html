Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes how SyncStream collects, uses, and shares your personal information.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect data necessary for providing and improving the SyncStream service, specifically for real-time synchronization and chat functionality.

  • **Account Data:** Username and email address (provided during registration).
  • **Usage Data:** Video IDs loaded, player state (play/pause/seek), and room membership.
  • **Communication Data:** Chat messages (transmitted peer-to-peer via our signaling server, but not permanently stored).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the real-time video synchronization service.
  • To manage your account and provide customer support.
  • To monitor usage to improve app stability and feature development.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only under the following circumstances:

  • **Service Providers:** With third-party partners (like cloud hosting for the signaling server) only to the extent necessary to run the service.
  • **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or government requests.

4. Security

The security of your personal data is paramount. All real-time video control data is transmitted using WebRTC, which is end-to-end encrypted. While we strive to protect your data, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information. Please contact us to exercise these rights.
